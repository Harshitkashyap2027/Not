GENERALHow do I reset a student's password?
▼
Go to User Management, find the student, click Edit and use the Reset Password option. An email will be sent to the student automatically.
GENERALHow do students access the platform?
▼
Students log in via the student portal at /auth/portal-login.html using their school email and password provided during registration.
TECHNICALWhy is the AI Sage not responding?
▼
Check if AI Sage is enabled in Settings > Features. If enabled but not working, check the API usage limits in Billing. Contact support if the issue persists.
TECHNICALHow do I fix video playback issues?
▼
Ensure the video files are under 500MB and in MP4/WebM format. Check the storage usage in Billing. Clear the CDN cache from Settings if needed.
PAYMENTHow is billing calculated?
▼
Billing is per active student seat per year. Seats are counted at the beginning of each billing period based on active student accounts.
PAYMENTCan I get a refund?
▼
Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase for unused seats. Contact billing@lvlbase.io with your invoice number.
ACCOUNTHow do I add a new admin user?
▼
Go to Team Management and click Add Member. Assign the appropriate role and permissions. The new admin will receive an invitation email.
ACCOUNTHow do I export student data?
▼
Go to Reports > Export CSV or User Management > Export CSV to download student data in CSV format for your records.
